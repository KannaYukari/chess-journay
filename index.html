In the land of the board, where kings do reign,

Derik marched forth, with a steady campaign.

But soon an old rival, with plans to unfold,

Eldrich came slowly, both daring and bold.

With courage and flair, a pawn leaped ahead,

Fred shouted loud, "For the fight, we are led!"

The board won’t be quiet—thrust and a dive—

Eldrich himself struck hard, keeping threats alive.

Then Niccolo, the knight, leaped to the fight,

And claimed where Eldrich had been in the light.

But cunning and sly, black pawns did conspire—

'Til Cyphus advanced, setting the board afire.

With dreams of a plan, White’s heart did implore,

Then clever Evans came, promising much more.

He brought forth new trades, forged treaties with flair,

But his peace was a façade, a trick to beware.
